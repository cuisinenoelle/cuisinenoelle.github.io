Lebanese Date Biscuits

Lebanese Date Biscuits


Main Ingredients:
  • 3 cups of four
  • 1 large spoon of yeast
  • 1 large spoon sugar
  • 1 small spoon baking powder
  • 0.25 small spoon of salt
  • 0.25 small spoon of cinnamon
  • 0.5 cup veggie oil
  • 1.25 cup of warm water
For Filling :
  • 450 grams date paste
  • 1 large spoon of butter
  • 1 small spoon of cinnamon
For Upper Layyer:
  • 1 large egg
  • 0.25 spoon small vanilla
  • 0.25 cup sesame seeds

Directions:

  1. In a bowl of your food processor with the dough hook add 2 1/2 cups of flour, yeast, baking powder, salt, cinnamon, and mix well.
  2. Add the oil and water and mix until dough separates from walls. Then add the remaining flour and knead for 5 minutes
  3. Remove and rub with oil, then place in an oiled bowl. Cover with plastic for 1 hour.
  4. Mix the filling ingredients until you get soft dough that you can shape into rings or Fingers.
  5. On a flat layer, roll out dough into any size you want (1-2 cm thickness) place filling, and roll over twice only. You can make one at a time or roll out a large sheet and use a knife to cut into your shapes.
  6. Place the fingers/fingers on a baking sheet. Mix the upper layer ingredients (egg, vanilla) and brush the fingers, then sprinkle with the sesame seeds. Place in a warm place for 10 minutes. ( I place on top of stove while oven is heating up)
  7. Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Place tray on middle rack and Bake for 10-20 until golden brown
  8. Remove and let cool before serving with coffee or tea.
