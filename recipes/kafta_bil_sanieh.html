Kafta Bil Sanieh

Kafta Bil Sanieh

Kafta patties topped with potatoes and tomatoes !

Ingredients :
  • Lean ground beef 500g
  • Grated onion 1/2 cup
  • Finely chopped fresh parsley 1 cup
  • Medium size potatoes, peeled and slice 3
  • Tomatoes cut in wedges or round 2
  • Small onion cut into rings and separated 1
  • Tomato sauce OR crushed tomato 3 cups
  • Water 3 cups
  • Black pepper 1 tsp
  • Oil for frying or baking 1/2 cup
  • Kafta spice powder 2 tsp
  • Salt 2 tsp or as desired

Directions:

  1. In a large bowl combine beef, grated onion, parsley, black pepper, kafta spice, half the salt( 1 1/2 tsp) and mix thoroughly with your hands until all ingredients are well combined.
  2. Divided mixture into equal size and shape into small patties. Place on an oven tray and bake for 15 minutes at 350 degrees or fry in pan with little oil until meat is cooked.
  3. Remove meat from tray and arrange into a deep dish oven tray. On top of meat patties layer the sliced potatoes, tomatoes and onions.
  4. In a bowl, mix the tomato sauce, water and remaining salt and pour evenly over the dish. Cover with foil and place in oven for 30 minutes or until potatoes are fork tender.
  5. Serve hot aside Lebanese rice
