Asian Beef Stir Fry with vegetables

Asian Beef Stir Fry

Asian Beef Stir Fry with vegetables !

Ingredients :
  • Vegetable oil ( one for each patch) 2 tbsp
  • Beef, thinly sliced or pre-sliced stir fry beef you can buy from the supermarket 500g
  • Chili oil 1 tbsp
  • Garlic, peeled and finely chopped 3 large cloves
  • Red chili, thinly sliced (more if you like it more spicy) 1
  • Sliced cremini mushrooms 1 cup
  • Baby sweet corns (about 117g), sliced in half 1 packet
  • Bok choy cleaned and roughly chopped 1.5 cup
  • Red pepper seeded and chopped 1
  • Yellow pepper seeded and chopped 1
  • Sliced celery 1 cup
  • Black pepper 1 tsp
  • Fish sauce – to taste 1 tbsp
  • Hoisin sauce 2 tbsp
  • Oyster sauce 2 tbsp
  • Soy sauce (low sodium) 0.5 cup
  • Lemon zest 1 tbsp
  • Finely chopped, peeled, fresh ginger 2 tsp

Directions:

  1. Heat oil in a large pan or wok, brown the beef in 2 batches to 80% cooked and set aside on a plate.
  2. In the same pan, add chili oil, garlic, ginger and sliced chilli and sauté over low heat until garlic becomes slightly light brown. Then add mushrooms and baby corn and cook for 1-2 minutes.
  3. Add the vegetables (bok choy, red and yellow pepper and celery) and stir-fry for 4 to 5 minutes.
  4. Return the beef to the pan and stir in fish sauce, black pepper, soy sauce, lemon zest hoisin and oyster sauce. Toss to combine all the ingredients. Cook for 2-3 minutes.
  5. Taste the beef and sauce. Adjust seasoning to achieve your desired taste.
  6. Serve immediately with coconut rice (see recipe).
