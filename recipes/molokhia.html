Molokhia

Molokhia (soup with chicken and jute leaves)


Main Ingredients:
  • Fresh or dry Melokhia leaves (pick through them and remove any stems) 500 grams
  • Fresh coriander, finely chopped 1 Cup
  • Fresh garlic roughly chopped 1/2 cup
  • Finely chopped green chili( seeded) 1 tbsp
  • Black Pepper 1 tsp
  • Ground coriander 1 tbsp
  • Lemon juice 1/2 tsp
  • Salt 1 tbsp
  • Oil 1/2 cup
Stock Ingredients:
  • Cinnamon stick 400 grams
  • Whole onion peeled and cut in half 2 Cups
  • Cardamom pods, crushed 3 tbsp
  • 1 whole chicken, cleaned and cut into 4-6 pieces 2 tbsp
  • 1 dried bay leaf 1 tsp
  • Whole cloves 2 tsp
  • Whole cardamom 1 tsp
  • Water 10 cups

Directions:

  1. In a pot, sear chicken in 1 tsp of oil skin side down, until golden brown. Place in cinnamon stick, bay leaf, whole cloves, onion, and cardamom, then pour water to cover the chicken while cooking on the stove.
  2. Cook on medium heat for 1 hour to make stock for Melokhie adjusting salt for desired taste.
  3. If using dry Melokhie leafs rehydrate in water for 20 minutes and give them a rinse to remove any debris, then place into a large pot with water (6-8 cups) and cook for 45 minutes while constantly turning, then drain water.
  4. If using fresh Melohkie leaves wash and rinse then fry in small patches and place side.
  5. Once you’re done, using the same pot, Sautee garlic, coriander and chili in 2 tbsp. of oil for 5 minutes on medium heat, then mix in Melokhia and lemon juice and cook for another 5 minutes.
  6. Using a strainer, carefully pour the stock over the Molokhia making sure nothing falls from stock.
  7. Remove chicken from stock and set aside to cool. Once cooled, separate chicken meat from bones and skin and add the last 10 minutes of cooking to Molokhia.
  8. Cover pot and cook over low heat for 1 hour or until Melokhie is tender. Last 10 minutes, add the shredded chicken and mix well.
  9. Remove and serve hot with your favourite white rice and lemon wedges.
