Sfouf (Turmeric Cake)

Ingredients :
  • 3 cups fine semolina flour
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1.75 cups of sugar
  • 1.5 Tbsp. of ground Turmeric
  • 2 cups warm milk
  • 2 tsp of baking powder
  • 1 cup of Canola oil
  • 1 tbsp. of Tahini paste
  • 0.5 cup of sliced almonds( for decoration-optional)
  • 2 Tbsp.Tahina (sesame paste)

Directions:

  1. Brush the bottom and sides of the pan 30 cm deep cake pan with Tahina paste
  2. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  3. In a bowl combine dry ingredients (flour, semolina, baking powder and turmeric and set aside.
  4. In a separate bowl, dissolve the milk and sugar, and then add the dry mixture and oil and beat on low-medium for 5 minutes (this step is very important).
  5. Pour the mixture in in greased pan.
  6. If using garnish on top then sprinkle with sliced almonds or pine nuts( Iâ€™ve make it with and without)
  7. Bake on middle rack of your oven for 30 minutes or until inserted wooden tooth pick come out clean.
  8. Remove and let to cool before serving. If not using a garnish, for an extra flavor and glistering top Brush the top layer with melted butter
